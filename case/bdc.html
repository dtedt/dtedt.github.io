Creating A High-Performing Medtech App with BDC


The Client

BDC was contracted to develop a new prototype, a redesign of an existing application that was simply not connecting with users as desired. Andrew pitched the idea of a gamified redesign, and now he was looking for a real game studio to bring an expert touch to this new prototype.

The Problem

The already existing application functioned, however it had low favorability with users and users frequently failed to engage with the app with the intended regularity (weekly check-ins). BDC already had a vision for an improved version of the application, but they needed seasoned game designers to implement the concept with special attention to interface design and interactivity.

The Solution

We implemented BDC's concept using the Unity engine in order to make it more dynamic and reactive. This enabled us to implement haptic feedback and [other features]

The Results

Once we placed the finishing touches on the prototype, it was sent out to participants in a trial run and the results of testing spoke for themself: 20x increase in re-engagement of dormant users, 3.4x increase in retention, 2.1x increase in tasks completed within the app.

Lessons Learned

Our collaboration with BDC was not merely a successful effort to produce an application. We effectively discovered and tested a new approach to medical communication technology that could potentially change the way doctors relate with patients about their condition.
